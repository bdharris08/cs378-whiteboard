A free and Efficient way to broadcast, record and replay classroom whiteboard.

Instructors and Students who use Papyrus love it. The reason stems from how and why we built Papyrus. The Papyrus allows instructors & students to collaborate together during the class sessions. The Papyrus software is designed to be hardware independent and will be able to interact with variety of devices. The result is a beautifully intuitive and simple product that both students and instructors love and use.Learn more ...

Why is Papyrus different?

Papyrus, in comparison to our competition, is easy to install and maintain. Papyrus will be instant, collaborative and able to record multiple lecture sessions. None of these features are currently available with our perceived competition.
